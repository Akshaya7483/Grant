UN Women Leadership Grant

Overview

This grant aims to support women leaders across various sectors globally by providing resources, mentorship, and financial aid to encourage impactful leadership. The grant amount is 150,000 USD.

Eligibility Criteria

Applicants must be women above the age of 18 and should have demonstrated leadership abilities in their respective fields. Applicants should also be actively involved in initiatives that promote gender equality and women’s empowerment.

Application Process

The application process includes submitting an online application form, a project proposal outlining the goals and impact of the initiative, and a personal essay discussing the applicant’s leadership journey.

Application Deadline

The deadline for the UN Women Leadership Grant is 31st December 2024. Applications received after this date will not be considered.

How to Apply

To apply for the UN Women Leadership Grant, visit the official grant page and complete the application form before the deadline.
